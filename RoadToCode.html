After nearly 5 years of combined experience in technology recruitment (currently at The Candidate), I have always found myself obsessed with the capabilities of technical people to understand and influence the technology which so many take for granted. In this blog, I'll be creating HTML documents inline with my Udemy course. Condering my love for Mathematics throughout my studies (A2+B2=C2), I'm truly surprised it's taken me this long to immerse myself into development although it's fair to say I'm not quite in love with every Developer's favourite chemical molecule yet; C8H10N4O2...
